Exploring the Landscapes of the Netherlands

Van Gogh Museum

Home to the world's largest collection of works by Vincent van Gogh, featuring over 200 paintings and 500 drawings.

Muiderslot Castle

A medieval castle dating back to 1285, surrounded by water and beautiful gardens.

Natural Landscapes

Despite being known as a flat country, the Netherlands features varied landscapes including rolling hills in the south.

Strategic Location

Located in Western Europe, the Netherlands borders Germany to the east, Belgium to the south, and the North Sea to the northwest.

"The Netherlands' strategic position at the mouth of several major European rivers has been key to its development as a trading nation."
